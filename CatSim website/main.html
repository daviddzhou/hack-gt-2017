HackGT Cat Simulator

This cat simulator is the accumulation of the combined efforts of CS-TA FIESTA in the 2017 HackGT event. Coming together to solved multiple barriers in not only learning new languages, but also working in new environemnts and facing stringent time constraints.

We present to you this game in the hopes that it can be realized that anything can be turned into a reality with the right people with you and the motivation to learn and succeed.
